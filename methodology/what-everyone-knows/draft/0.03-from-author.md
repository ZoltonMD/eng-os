## From Author

You can think of this book as a framework. This isn't a set of rules you must meekly follow. It's a set of tools you can use to achieve maximum results. Some of them may be applicable in some situations and not in others. Some work for some people but not for others. Some require some tuning in certain situations.

I can't just tell you what you must do to be more productive and more valuable. There is no magical recipe which makes you a better copy of yourself. My goal is to show you what tools you already have, how and when you can use them, and in which directions they can be tuned .

English is not my first language and that was another point for me. I wanted to make this book accessible for people like me, for whom English is not their first language. And these are two reasons why I tried to write this book using simple words and sentences. I hope you'll love it.

That's all I wanted to say before we start. I have no right to waste more of your time.
