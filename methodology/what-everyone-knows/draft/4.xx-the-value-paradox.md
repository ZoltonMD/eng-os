# The Value Paradox: Engineering Integrity vs. Budget Cynicism
I once had a CTO tell me that the engineering department is "just a cost." The logic was that engineers don't generate revenue like sales or account management do.  
Back then, I lost my motivation. *"Can an engineer value the CTO who does not value his engineers?"* I thought.  
I can't agree with his point of view. Let's run a simple mental health check: *"What is the revenue of your top-tier sales team when system uptime is zero?"*. Exactly. It’s zero. Revenue is not a constant; it is a function of uptime $(U)$ and performance $(P)$.

$$Revenue=f(Presenters)×Uptime(Engineering)$$

If $Uptime=0$, then $Revenue=0$.
